CM Roshan Gharana Solar Boards Scheme

Overview

The CM Roshan Gharana Program, led by Chief Minister Maryam Nawaz, aims to provide solar panels to residents of Punjab to help them cope with high power costs and frequent load shedding during the summer season.

This initiative will allow individuals to generate their own electricity, significantly reducing their electricity bills.

Program Details

Aspect Details
Program CM Roshan Gharana Program Solar Board Scheme
Components Inverters, Batteries, Advanced Solar Plates
Down Payment 25% of the total cost by the recipient
Allocation Method Open Lottery System
Government Subsidy The remaining 75% of the total cost

Stage I of the program will start soon with a budget allocation of 12.6 billion rupees, providing solar systems to 50,000 homes. The total value of the solar panels will be between 2 to 3 lakhs, with a 25% down payment required from the beneficiary.

Benefits

Using solar panels means free electricity, significantly reducing the amount paid in utility bills. Qualified individuals will receive 2-3 solar panels, each of 1KW, along with necessary installation services at no extra cost.

Eligibility Criteria

Criteria Description
Electricity Usage Using 100 units of electricity per month
Residency Resident of Punjab
Verification Electricity bill, mobile number, and national identity card will be checked

How to Apply

To apply for the solar board registration:

  1. Visit Punjab Bank or Meezan Bank with your mobile number, CNIC, and electricity bill.
  2. Request the registration form for the Roshan Gharana Scheme.
  3. Fill out the form with accurate details and submit it.
  4. Wait for verification and, if eligible, you will be notified to collect your solar panels.
  5. Submit the 25% down payment at the specified location to receive your solar panels.

Distribution Process

The government will check data from WAPDA to identify families using 100 units or less per month. If the number of qualified families exceeds 50,000, a lottery draw will determine the recipients.

Conclusion

The CM Roshan Gharana Solar Boards Scheme, led by Maryam Nawaz Sharif, provides a valuable opportunity for residents of Punjab to reduce their electricity costs and cope with high summer temperatures through solar energy.

Eligible individuals can register through Punjab Bank and Meezan Bank to benefit from this initiative and enjoy cost-free solar energy.
